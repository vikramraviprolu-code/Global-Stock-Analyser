Tiered peer matching

Peers found through a 6-step fallback so every stock gets a comparable cohort.

1 Same industry + country — closest comp
2 Same industry + region — regional peers
3 Same sector + country — domestic sector
4 Same sector + region — regional sector
5 Global same-industry — worldwide comp
6 Global sector — final fallback

How it works

1

Enter a ticker

Type any US-listed symbol. Hit Analyze.

2

We pull free data

Stooq CSV → yfinance fallback for OHLCV. yfinance for fundamentals. No keys, no scraping.

3

Indicators computed locally

52W high/low, 5D perf, MAs, RSI-14, ROC-14/21, avg volume — calculated server-side from raw bars.

4

Peers screened

Same sector/industry from curated universe. Global filters: price ≥ $5, vol ≥ 500K, mcap ≥ $2B.

5

Score + recommendation

Transparent +1/-1 rules across value, momentum, and risk penalties. Final call: Buy / Watch / Avoid.

Transparent scoring — see every point

No black-box rating. Every increment is visible in the Recommendation tab.

Value Score /4

  • +1 within 10% of 52W low
  • +1 trailing P/E ≤ 10
  • +1 market cap ≥ $2B
  • +1 attractive vs peer median P/E

Momentum Score /7

  • +1 5D positive
  • +1 ROC 14D positive
  • +1 ROC 21D positive
  • +1 RSI between 40 and 70
  • +1 price above 20D MA
  • +1 price above 50D MA
  • +1 price above 200D MA

Risk Penalties

  • -1 RSI > 70 (overbought)
  • -1 price below 200D MA
  • -1 ROC 14D & 21D both negative
  • ↓ confidence reduced if data missing
